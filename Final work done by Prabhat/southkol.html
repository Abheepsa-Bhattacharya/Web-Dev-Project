6 Ballygunge Place, Kolkata

In terms of buffet restaurants in Kolkata, 6 Ballygunge Place is a local favorite and is often mentioned in connection with the Ilish Festival. Chef Sushanta Sengupta creates a special menu of mouthwatering treats just for the celebration. The celebration wouldn't be complete without sampling the fishes that can be found at 6 Ballygunge Place, which perfectly matches the seasonal availability of fish. For fish enthusiasts in particular, the festival is not the only occasion to visit this eatery.
